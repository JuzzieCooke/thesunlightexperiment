Zolpidem

Zolpidem is a GABA agonist, but is NOT a benzodiazepine. it binds selectively to GABA BZ1 receptors rather than all BZ receptors like benzodiazepines. As a result it causes only sedation, and no anxiolytic, and anticonvulsant activity.

Used for the short term treatment of insomnia only (up to 4 weeks)
